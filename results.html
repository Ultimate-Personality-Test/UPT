NOTE: The test is meant not to give you a definitive type, but rather tell you how well you match each type. It is possible that your actual type will not be the top option, but it is likely to be one of the top. Note also that when it comes to the enneagram, the results might seem to contradict themselves. The "type" may tell you you're enneagram 1, while "wing" tells you you're a 2, "tritype" gives you a core 3 and "family" will say it's 4-5-9. Or maybe you got 7w8, despite your 6 score being greater than the 8 one (and similar things with the tritype, or the family). This is because each enneagram sub-system is calculated semi-separately.
